About

// 01

Ever since I was a kid, I've been fascinated by how technology works, from taking apart a broken Nintendo DS to poking around inside the code of the video games I loved. That curiosity grew into a passion for coding that I'm still chasing today.

I'm pursuing it as a Computer Science and Applied Statistics double major at the University of Illinois at Chicago. Computer science was the natural starting point; I added Applied Statistics after a data science workshop made me realize how much I wanted to use data to aid research and answer real questions.

Outside of class I learn new languages, build projects, and stay active in student organizations. I'm a member of LOGICA (the Latinx Organization of Growth and Computer Analytics) and the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). I'm looking for opportunities in software engineering or data science where I can contribute to impactful projects and keep growing as both a developer and an analyst.
